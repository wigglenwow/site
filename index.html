I Hope it felt Calm.

Welcome to my space. I'm Aryan Kumar Pawan, an artist and creator who finds peace in the process of making things by hand.

WigglenWow started as a place to document my experiments with different art mediums. I believe that the medium is just as important as the subject. I love the messy, unpredictable nature of traditional art—the smudge of willow charcoal, the slow drying time of watercolor and oil paint, the tactile feel of a good sketchbook.

This portfolio is a collection of my finished works, my messy experiments, and the thoughts in between.

A Current Thought

"I've been thinking a lot about the spaces between things lately. Not the objects themselves, but the air around them. How silence isn't empty, but full of anticipation. Sometimes, the most important part of a piece is the space you choose to leave bare."
